Why this exists

Prague's souvenir shops
are not Czech

Research shows that most souvenir shops on Prague's Royal Way are owned and staffed by non-Czechs. The connection to local culture is minimal. You leave with a magnet made in China.

We believe Czech culture deserves better. Every item in our boxes is made in the Czech Republic, chosen for its story, and accompanied by context that makes it meaningful — not just decorative.
